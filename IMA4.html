IMA 4 - Linear Course

Proposal/Analysis

This course is designed  to help understand the spread of germs and pathogens. Learners are given opportunities to apply the new  knowledge throughout the course.  

This project has educational value because with the current COVID-19 pandemic infecting millions worldwide, it is more important than ever  to understand how germs work. By completing this course, learners can understand what germs are and how they spread.

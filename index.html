Welcome!       

My name is Kara Kelley. I am a graduate student in the Learning and Design Technology program at the University of South Florida.

This site is a collection of my designs for EME6208 - Interactive Media with Dr. Sanghoon Park. These designs were created in the Fall 2020 semester at the University of South Florida.

The first project, IMA 1, is a Job Aid designed with Photoshop. The second project, IMA 2, is a  usability test conducted to evaluate a popular language learning app. The third project, IMA 3, is an instructional video  designned with iMovie. The final project, IMA 4, is a linear branched learning course designed with Captivate.                

I hope you enjoy my creations!

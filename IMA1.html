IMA 1 - Job performance Aid 

Job aids are graphics that are designed to help quickly solve a task.  

Before designing a job aid, it is necessary to complete a performance analysis first.  A designer needs to know what problem they are trying to solve before they can begin the design process.

Click here for a flowchart that can help analyze a performance problem.                

The next step is determining if a job aid is the support  or training that will be most beneficial. Job aids are appropriate for infrequent, non-crucial tasks. They are most helpful when complex information is rapidly changing but full scale training is not necessary.

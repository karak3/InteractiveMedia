IMA 3 - Instructional video    

For this project, I made an instructional video using Keynote and iMovie.

This instructional video teaches about the use of Total Physical Response (TPR). This video is valuable because TPR is an important strategy that teachers can use to help 2nd language learners. This video cann help educators understand what TPR is and how to implement it into their classrooms.
